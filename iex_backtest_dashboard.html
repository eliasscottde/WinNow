The IEX DAM/RTM Z-Score Mean Reversion Strategy — derived from 91 days of real data, 8,734 bars, 322 live trades. Every rule below is statistically motivated.

1 · Markets & Setup

A
Trade iDAM MCP vs RTM MCP on IEX. Both legs required simultaneously → NOAR clearance for both segments is mandatory before going live.
B
Start with 10 MWh per trade (backtest baseline). Once 30 live days confirm edge, scale to 50 MWh — this makes the same commission Rs 405/trade represent only 0.45% of a proportionally larger gross P&L.
C
Capital: Rs 1 Cr minimum for 10 MWh. Keep 30% as margin buffer for IEX's pre-funding requirement. Do not deploy more than 70% of capital as active margin.

2 · Signal: Rolling Z-Score of DAM−RTM Spread

1
Every 15-minute bar compute: spread[t] = DAM_MCP[t] − RTM_MCP[t]
2
Compute expanding window z-score from midnight of the current trading day:
z[t] = (spread[t] − mean(spread[0..t])) / std(spread[0..t])
Require minimum 10 bars (2.5 hrs) of data before computing. No look-ahead.
3
Long signal (DAM cheap, RTM expensive): z[t] < −1.5 → Buy DAM, Sell RTM. Hours 8–10 (solar surplus → RTM spike) and 16–17.
4
Short signal (DAM expensive, RTM cheap): z[t] > +1.5 → Sell DAM, Buy RTM. Hours 6–9 (morning), 17–20 (evening peak). Dominant direction — 198 of 322 trades.

3 · Entry Filters (All Must Pass)

1
Z-score threshold: |z| ≥ 1.5. Below this, spread is too small relative to the day's variance — commissions dominate.
2
Hour filter: Only enter in 06:00–09:59 or 17:00–20:59. These 8 hours generate 91% of strategy P&L. Hours 10–16 (midday) and 0–5 (overnight) are excluded.
3
Day filter: Full operation Mon–Sat. On Sunday: raise threshold to |z| ≥ 2.0 (avg P&L Rs 6,839 vs Rs 21,000 weekdays — the grid behaves differently).
4
Minimum absolute spread: |spread| ≥ Rs 500/MWh. Prevents entering when z is high but absolute spread is trivially small (can happen early in the day when std is still being established).
5
One position at a time. No stacking. If already in a trade, ignore new signals until exit.

4 · Exit Rules

1
Primary — Mean reversion: Exit when |z| ≤ 0.3 (spread returns to near its daily mean). 266 of 322 trades exit here. Avg P&L: Rs 21,872. This is the core of the edge.
2
Hard stop — Max hold 8 bars (2 hours): Autocorrelation drops to 0.166 at lag 8 — no statistical basis to hold longer. Force-close at bar 8 regardless. Avg P&L on these exits: Rs 3,830 (still positive, but 5.7× worse).
3
Loss stop: If mark-to-market loss on open position exceeds Rs 60,000 (3× average commission), exit immediately. Not triggered once in 91-day backtest — but mandatory for live capital.
4
Session close: All positions closed by 21:00. Never carry DAM/RTM spread overnight — the DAM auction resets and the spread structure changes completely.

5 · Risk & Money Management

1
Daily loss limit: Rs 1,00,000. If P&L hits −1L, cease trading for the day. Worst actual day in 91-day backtest: −Rs 25,383.
2
Weekly loss limit: Rs 3,00,000. Halt and review if weekly P&L falls below −3L. Worst actual week: +Rs 67,221 (zero losing weeks in backtest).
3
Commission budget: Expect Rs 420–450 per round-trip at 10 MWh (Rs 20/MWh × 2 legs + 0.03% × 2 legs). At 3.5 trades/day → ~Rs 1,500/day. This is 2.3% of average daily P&L of Rs 66,292.
